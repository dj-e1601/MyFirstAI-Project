# MyFirstAI-Project
Learning AI
The COSTAR Mnemonic: Familiarize yourself with the components: Context,
Objective, Style, Tone, Audience, and Response.
Examples Matter: Briefly consider how providing a model with a "pattern" (OneShot prompting) can lead to much more accurate results than just asking a
question (Zero-Shot).
Neutrality is Key: Understand why "leading the witness" in a prompt can cause
AI to cherry-pick data or hallucinate to please you.
Research Barriers: Reflect on how much time you currently spend searching
through multiple Google tabs or hitting paywalls; we will be solving these
inefficiencies.
Pro-Tip: If you have a specific complex topic you've been wanting to research
(e.g., "The impact of EVs on the environment"), have it ready to use during the
live demonstration!
